    There are many different ways to get to the same place. Johannesburg is a big place, but once you learn to connect the dots,it can give you the courage to navigate the city. Intuitively guiding users along their trip our app will involve first selecting an appropriate location as well as the preffered destination. Based on the selection, the app will provide the correct hand signal for that particular location. The user will be able to learn and practice different signs of the location around the city without asking around.
